About the Schwab API

The Charles Schwab API allows Wheeler to connect directly to your brokerage account for portfolio data and trading operations.

  • App Key: Your application's client ID, obtained from the Schwab Developer Portal
  • Secret Key: Your application's client secret, kept confidential
  • Access: Register at developer.schwab.com to create an app and obtain credentials

Your credentials are stored locally in the Wheeler database and are never transmitted to third parties.
